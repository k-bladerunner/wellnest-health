Our Story

Founded in 1998 with a vision to transform healthcare

From Small Clinic to Healthcare Leader

WellNest Health was founded by Dr. Amanda Rodriguez with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a healthcare facility that truly cares about its patients beyond just treating illnesses.

What started as a small clinic with just 5 staff members has grown into a comprehensive healthcare network serving over 50,000 patients annually across multiple specialties.

Our approach combines cutting-edge medical technology with the personal touch that healthcare should always have. We believe in treating the whole person, not just symptoms.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare that addresses the root causes of health issues and empowers individuals to live their healthiest lives.

We believe in treating the whole person with compassion, using advanced diagnostics and personalized treatment plans.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider recognized for innovation, compassionate care, and measurable patient outcomes.

We envision a future where preventive care and personalized treatment are the standard, not the exception.
